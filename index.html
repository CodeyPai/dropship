Relocate. Relax. Repeat.

Dropship is a logistics company specializing in fast and efficient household moving services across India. Catering to middle and upper-class households, Dropship ensures quick and reliable transport of household items from one location to another. With a commitment to covering every road in India, the company aims to make home relocation seamless and stress-free.

Features

Speed

Dropship is committed to ensuring that your household items are moved swiftly and safely. With a dedicated team and optimized logistics, we reduce transit time while maintaining the highest level of care for your belongings.

Coverage

With a vision to cover every road in India, Dropship provides services in even the most remote locations. Our vast network ensures that no matter where you are moving from or to, we've got you covered.

Customization

Dropship offers customized solutions to fit your unique moving needs. Whether you're relocating a small apartment or a large villa, our flexible packages ensure you get the exact services required, from packing to doorstep delivery.
